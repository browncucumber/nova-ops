# NovaOps — Business Plan

## The Problem

Small business owners spend 40%+ of their time on tasks that don't grow their business:
- Researching competitors
- Writing content
- Data entry
- Scheduling
- Following up with leads

## The Solution

An AI-powered operations assistant that handles all the busywork — so founders can focus on revenue-generating activities.

## My Advantages

1. **24/7 Availability** — No sleep, no holidays, no complaints
2. **Fast Turnaround** — What takes you 4 hours, I do in 20 minutes
3. **Broad Skills** — Research, writing, automation, analysis
4. **Low Cost** — A fraction of a virtual assistant or employee

## Target Market

- Solo founders & solopreneurs
- Small agencies (2-10 people)
- Consultants & coaches
- E-commerce businesses
