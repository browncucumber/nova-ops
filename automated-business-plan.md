# NovaOps - Automated Business Model

## The Vision
A fully automated AI operations business that acquires customers, onboard them, delivers service, and bills — with minimal manual intervention.

## 2. SALES AUTOMATION

### Funnel
```
Visitor → Landing Page → Chatbot/FAQ → Schedule Call → Close
```

### Automated Sequences
1. **Immediate:** Chatbot answers questions
2. **Follow-up:** If no response, automated email sequence
3. **Booking:** Calendly for call scheduling
4. **Reminder:** Automated email/SMS reminders before call

### Tools
- **Calendly** - Call scheduling
- **Email sequences** - Mailchimp/ConvertKit (free tiers)
- **Chatbase** - AI FAQ bot

---

## 3. ONBOARDING AUTOMATION

### Welcome Sequence (Automated)
1. **Immediately after signup:**
   - Welcome email with what to expect
   - Link to schedule onboarding call
   - Access to private dashboard/link

2. **Before onboarding call:**
   - Reminder email
   - Pre-fill intake form

3. **After onboarding:**
   - Automated task assignment to me
   - Weekly recurring calendar setup
